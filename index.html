• Cable Co.

A good day starts with a great coffee.

We can’t promise coffee will solve all life’s complications, but it’s a decent place to start. Fuelling the Kensal Rise, Hammersmith and Kensington communities since 2014, our independently owned coffee shops are famous for their friendly atmosphere, tasty treats and of course, top-notch coffee.

Whether you’re looking for a place to meet a friend, or need to ‘work from home’ but not in the house, our cafes are here for you. Grab an Americano on the go or mull over the papers with an oat milk latte, Cable.Co is here for wherever the day may take you.
